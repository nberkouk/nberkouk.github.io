What ?

    The theory of persistence was developed in the early 2000’s as an attempt to define topological descriptors of datasets which are stable to noise - the so-called barcodes. Persistence theory has been studied and used in different mathematical contexts: homotopy theory, sheaves, symplectic geometry... The aim of this seminar is to gather the mathematical communities who have a common interest in the theoretical aspects of persistence.
